Our Story

A 2,000-Year-Old Tradition.
An Australian First.

It started with a simple observation: the Himalayan dog chew — one of the most popular natural pet treats in the world — wasn't being made in Australia.

Every bag on Australian shelves was imported. Made somewhere else, shipped across oceans, with supply chains that are opaque at best.

KeKo Pet was founded to change that. We studied the traditional churpi-making process used by communities across Nepal and the Himalayas for centuries — a method of slowly pressing and air-drying milk into a dense, long-lasting chew. We adapted that process for an Australian context, using fresh local milk, and began producing in Melbourne, Victoria.

We are not a reseller. We are not an importer. We make what we sell.

The KeKo Difference

Why Australian?

Australian dairy is among the cleanest, most traceable in the world. We know our suppliers by name. We know where the milk comes from. That transparency carries through to every chew.

Why Air-Dried?

Air-drying at low temperatures preserves the natural protein structure of milk. It's slow, it's patient, and it produces a chew that dogs work at for hours — not minutes. High heat kills nutrients. We don't use it.

Why No Additives?

The Himalayan churpi is made from milk, lime juice, and salt. Three ingredients. It has worked for 2,000 years. We saw no reason to add anything else.

Our Vision
