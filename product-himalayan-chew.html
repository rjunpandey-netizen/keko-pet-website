The KeKo Himalayan Style Dog Chew is Australia's first locally produced milk-based dog chew. Made using fresh Australian dairy, air-dried slowly using the traditional Himalayan churpi method.

Unlike imported chews, every KeKo chew is traceable to its source — Victorian dairy. No artificial hardeners. No preservatives. No colours. Just milk, lime juice, and salt — the same recipe Himalayan communities have trusted for over two thousand years.

  • Single source: Australian milk
  • Air-dried — never baked, never extruded
  • High protein, low fat, low odour
  • Long-lasting — designed for hours of chewing
  • No preservatives, no artificial ingredients
  • Suitable for dogs 6 months and older
  • Not suitable for dogs with dairy sensitivities

Usage Instructions

Give to your dog and supervise. When the chew becomes small enough to swallow whole, remove it. You can microwave the small end piece for 60 seconds — it puffs up into a crunchy snack your dog will love.

Ingredients

Australian whole milk, food-grade lime juice, salt.

Common Questions

Is it safe for puppies?

Suitable for puppies 6 months and older with supervision.

Move up a size for stronger or larger chewers. Our Medium and Large sizes are built for extended chewing sessions.

Can I give this to a dog with a sensitive stomach?

Consult your vet if your dog has known dairy sensitivities.

What is churpi?
