The Chew That Started It All

KeKo Himalayan Style Dog Chew

For centuries, mountain communities across Nepal and the Himalayas have made churpi — a hard cheese chew crafted from yak and cow milk. Dense, slow-drying, and built for hours of sustained chewing.

We took that tradition and brought it to Australia. KeKo's Himalayan Style Dog Chew is made from fresh Australian milk, air-dried slowly in our Melbourne facility. No cooking. No shortcuts. Just the ancient method — done the Australian way.
